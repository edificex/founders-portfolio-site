Hi, my name is Dan

Listicles seem to be the height of fashion, so here are five fast facts about me:

  1. I've been hanging around on this particular turtle for about 25 years
  2. I grew up in east London, but have made my home for the past few years in Haringey, north London
  3. I get coin by working as a receptionist at a rock climbing centre down Green Lanes
  4. I like to read speculative sci-fi, play the drums, go to dance parties and tinker with tech
  5. I've been involved in left wing political organising for many years and believe another world is possible

So that's a quick rundown. If you'd like to know more about what I've been up to recently, just click a button!
